Photo to SVG Art

I love a good challenge, and photo to SVG conversions are one of my favorite kind! The mission here is to take a low-resolution image file, typically a JPG, and create a custom Scalable Vector Graphic (SVG) version.

Why on earth would I do this? SVGs are infinitely scalable. You can resize an SVG to be as small as a coffee mug design or as large as a billboard, and they will be crisp as can be. Oh, the possibilities, right?
