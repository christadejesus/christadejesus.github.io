Welcome to my design gallery!

I definitely have an enthusiasm for visual art and digital design that began back in my days as a professional photographer — actually, it probably took root in 5th grade art class! While I still love photography, painting, and other forms of artistic expression, I now gravitate toward creating digital designs with Inkscape, Adobe Photoshop, Canva, and DALL·E.

Often, I create scalable vector graphics (SVGs), typically used for logos, web graphics, and print products, as shown below. However, I also enjoy special projects such as converting low-resolution photos to SVGs, as well as custom graphic design and image editing.
